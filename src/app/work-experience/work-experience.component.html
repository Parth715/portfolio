Once I moved to Ohio I worked for Allegion Steel Craft, a steel door and frame manufacturing company. I worked from December 2nd 2020 to September 13th 2021. Essentially what I did was weld small parts onto frames that came down the line. I also had to make sure what my co-workers welded on was in accordance to the job description. Once quality was good and I got all the pieces I needed to make a complete door frame I would hang the frames on a conveyer. My last reponsibility was to make sure our production line reached the production goal for the day. I would say this job taught me how to communicate and work well with others.

Along with that I was in charge with managing the businesses inventory and training new employees. My main responsibility was to provide exceptional customer support and be able to grow sales. This job had taught me how to convey what I was thinking and be able to handle high pressure situations.
